01 · Live CVR / CTC

Reconciliation that doesn't wait for month-end.

Margin pulls cost from your ERP and value from your applications every 15 minutes. CVR and CTC stay accurate between reporting cycles, so variance shows up the day it appears — not the week it's certified.

  • Automatic accruals from PO commitments
  • MOS, over/under-measure flagged on entry
  • Final margin forecast updates as cost lands
02 · Cash flow

Forecasts that hold up at board level.

Project-level S-curves roll up to a portfolio cash position. Negative-position weeks light up 90 days before they happen, with the contributing projects ranked by exposure.

  • Planned vs anticipated vs actual on one chart
  • Advance payment recovery scheduled automatically
  • Retention release tracking, no spreadsheets

FAQ

Questions buyers actually ask.

How does Margin integrate with our existing ERP?
Native connectors for SAP, Oracle, and Sage. REST API for everything else. Implementation team handles the schema mapping — you don't move data manually.
Where is our data hosted?
UK only. AWS London region. Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Tenant isolation at the application and database layer.
How long is implementation?
4–6 weeks for the first three projects. Our implementation team is ex-commercial-management — they speak your language and know your ERP.
Do you support FIDIC and NEC?
Yes — both contract families. Notice templates, clock tracking, and entitlement workflows match the clauses you actually use.
What does success look like in 90 days?
CVR reconciled to within 1% of audit, weekly. Cash forecast variance under 5%. At least one variation captured in week one that previously would have been missed.
